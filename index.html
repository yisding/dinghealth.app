Our goal is to put you back in the driver's seat of your health.

Ding Health is inspired by our founders’ own experiences dealing with the healthcare system. We felt like we were along for a rollercoaster ride of 15 minute doctor’s visits, scrambling to remember our symptoms and the doctor’s recommendation, and mystery-box hospital bills afterwards.

Your Privacy is Our Top Priority

Data privacy is one of our most important principles. Your data is encrypted with industry leading AES encryption on your device, in transit, and at rest. We don't sell your data, ever.
